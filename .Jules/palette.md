## 2025-05-15 - [Refined Edit/Delete Interaction]
**Learning:** Transitioning between "Add" and "Edit" modes should be visually explicit. Changing the primary action button's icon/color and providing a "Cancel" option significantly reduces user confusion and accidental overwrites. Replacing disruptive alerts with non-blocking notifications (Toastify) improves the overall flow.
**Action:** Always provide a clear way to exit specialized modes (like editing) and update the primary action button to reflect the current state.
